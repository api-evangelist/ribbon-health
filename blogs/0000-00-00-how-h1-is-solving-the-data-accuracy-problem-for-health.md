H1 addresses data accuracy challenges for health plans by aggregating fragmented provider data from multiple sources, normalizing it through AI-powered roster automation, and assigning confidence scores to ensure regulatory compliance. The company reports improving record accuracy from roughly 59% to 90%-plus confidence.
