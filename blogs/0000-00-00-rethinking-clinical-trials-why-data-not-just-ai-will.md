A panel of clinical trial leaders argues that high-quality, structured data, rather than AI capabilities alone, will be the key differentiator in advancing clinical trials. The panelists emphasize that while AI tools are becoming commoditized, access to validated clinical data remains scarce and difficult to replicate.
